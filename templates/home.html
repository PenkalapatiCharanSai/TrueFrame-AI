About

About TrueFrame AI

🛡️

What Is TrueFrame AI?

TrueFrame AI is a video authenticity detection platform built to help journalists, researchers, legal professionals, and everyday people verify whether a video is genuine or AI-generated. In an era of widespread synthetic media, TrueFrame AI provides a fast, reliable, forensic-grade verdict with a single upload.

🎯

Mission

To fight misinformation at its source — making deepfake and AI-generated video detection accessible to everyone, not just specialists. We believe truth should be verifiable by all.

⚙️

Dual-Model Technology

Two independent neural networks run in parallel — one specialised in face-swap artifacts, another in generative model signatures. Both vote on every frame for a final confident verdict.

🔒

Privacy First

Uploaded videos are processed ephemerally. Files are auto-deleted within 2 minutes of analysis. PDF reports are deleted immediately after download. Your data stays yours.
